Explore the fascinating world of neuroscience through curated facts that bridge rigorous research with everyday wonder.

The Speed of Thought

Information travels along different types of neurons at different speeds. Some signals can move as fast as 268 miles per hour (431 km/h)—faster than a Formula 1 racing car.

water_drop VITAL STAT

73% Water

Your brain is about 73% water. Even a 2% dehydration can affect your attention, memory, and other cognitive skills.

Neuroplasticity

Your brain never stops changing. Every new skill creates a new neural connection.

Power of a Bulb

The human brain generates about 23 watts of electrical power while awake—enough to illuminate a small LED light bulb.

Did you know about the Gut-Brain Axis?

Often called the "second brain," the enteric nervous system in your gut contains over 100 million neurons. This complex highway influences your mood, immune system, and decision-making long before your conscious mind realizes it.

2.5 Petabytes

The storage capacity of the human brain is estimated to be roughly 2,500,000 gigabytes—equivalent to 300 years of continuous HD video.

nightlight_round RECOVERY

Sleep Cleaning

During sleep, the glymphatic system opens up, allowing cerebrospinal fluid to "wash" the brain of toxic waste products accumulated during the day.

face_retouching_natural RECOGNITION

The Face Region

The fusiform gyrus is a specialized part of the brain dedicated solely to recognizing faces. We are evolutionarily hardwired for social connection.
